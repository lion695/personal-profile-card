HGV Driver TO TECH - How I made the career change and why.

Hi, I'm Kieron, a Junior Full-Stack Web Developer. Following a successful career as an HGV driver, I made the strategic decision to pivot into the technology sector to pursue my passion for software engineering and embrace a more dynamic, growth-oriented career path.

The transition demanded rigorous dedication. Through disciplined study, the support of industry mentors, and an active learning community, I have successfully built a robust foundation in modern web development technologies.

Crucial to this journey has been my time at Code Institute, where I am currently completing their intensive Full Stack Web Development program. This curriculum has provided me with the structured technical expertise, practical project experience, and professional confidence required to excel in the industry.

I am now actively seeking new opportunities to apply my skills, contribute to an engineering team, and continue my professional growth. I am equally passionate about sharing my story to help inspire and guide other career changers looking to enter tech.
